Art

About Art:

Art class for lower school students nurtures creativity and self-expression through a variety of fun and engaging projects. Students explore different mediums like drawing, painting, and sculpting, while learning about famous artists and their techniques. The curriculum includes hands-on activities that foster fine motor skills and imaginative thinking. Collaborative projects and art appreciation lessons enhance teamwork and cultural awareness. Overall, art class encourages a lifelong love for the arts and creative exploration.

Curriculum Details:

The art curriculum for all classes includes foundational skills in drawing, painting, and sculpting, progressing to more complex techniques as students advance. It emphasizes creativity, critical thinking, and art appreciation, with projects that explore different cultures and art history. Collaborative and individual projects help students express themselves and develop fine motor skills. The curriculum fosters a lifelong appreciation for the arts and encourages creative exploration.

Activities:

Art activities for students include drawing and painting to enhance creativity and fine motor skills. Crafting projects like collages and sculptures foster imaginative thinking and hands-on learning. Interactive projects, such as group murals and seasonal art, encourage collaboration and cultural appreciation.

Art in :

Our school's art classes are distinguished by a focus on creative expression and individual growth. We offer a diverse range of mediums and techniques, encouraging students to explore and develop their unique artistic talents. With dedicated instructors and a supportive environment, our program nurtures a lifelong appreciation for the arts.
