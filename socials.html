Social Studies

About Social Studies:

Social science is the study of human society and social relationships, encompassing disciplines like history, geography, economics, and civics. It helps students understand cultural, political, and economic systems, fostering critical thinking and informed citizenship. Through engaging activities and real-world applications, students learn about societal structures and global interconnections. The curriculum aims to develop responsible, empathetic individuals who contribute positively to their communities.

Curriculum Details:

The social science curriculum for lower school students covers fundamental topics in history, geography, civics, and economics. It aims to build an understanding of societal structures, cultural diversity, and geographical features. Through engaging lessons and activities, students learn about significant historical events, basic economic principles, and the functions of government. The curriculum fosters critical thinking and informed citizenship, preparing students to be active and responsible members of society.

Activities:

Social science activities for students include role-playing historical events to understand significant moments and figures. Map-making and geographical explorations foster spatial awareness and cultural understanding. Interactive projects like class debates on civic issues and economic simulations help develop critical thinking and informed citizenship.

Social studies in :

Our school's social studies program excels in fostering a deep understanding of cultural, historical, and civic concepts through interactive and immersive learning experiences. Students engage in dynamic projects, debates, and role-playing activities that make historical events and societal structures come alive. Emphasis is placed on critical thinking, empathy, and informed citizenship, preparing students to be active and responsible members of their communities. Our dedicated educators ensure that each student develops a well-rounded perspective on global and local issues.
