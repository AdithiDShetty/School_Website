Languages

About Languages:

Our school's language program encompasses English, Kannada, and Hindi, providing a well-rounded linguistic education. English classes focus on reading, writing, speaking, and comprehension skills, fostering global communication. Kannada lessons highlight regional language and cultural heritage, enhancing local connections. Hindi classes build proficiency in India's widely spoken national language, facilitating broader communication across the country. Together, these languages promote multilingualism and cultural appreciation among students.

Curriculum Details:

The language curriculum in our school covers English, Kannada, and Hindi, aiming to develop comprehensive linguistic skills. Activities:

Language club activities include interactive sessions like storytelling and drama to enhance speaking skills and creativity. Cultural immersion days celebrate regional traditions and languages, fostering appreciation and understanding. Debate and public speaking events sharpen argumentative and presentation skills. Additionally, language games and quizzes make learning fun and engaging, encouraging teamwork and collaboration.
