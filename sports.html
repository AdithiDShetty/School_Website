Physical Education

About Sports:

Physical education is essential for the holistic development of students, emphasizing physical fitness, motor skills, and overall health. It promotes teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship through various activities such as games, sports, and exercises. PE classes help in reducing stress and improving concentration and academic performance. Through structured and enjoyable activities, physical education nurtures both the mind and body.

Curriculum Details:

The physical education curriculum for lower school students focuses on developing physical fitness, motor skills, and healthy habits. Activities include structured games, sports, and exercises that promote coordination, strength, and endurance. Emphasis is placed on teamwork, sportsmanship, and fair play. Lessons on basic anatomy and the importance of physical activity are included to enhance understanding. The curriculum aims to instill a love for physical activity, reducing stress, and improving overall well-being. By engaging in regular physical activity, students develop habits that contribute to lifelong health and fitness.

Activities:

Physical education activities for students include a variety of engaging games and exercises to promote fitness and teamwork. These activities often feature traditional sports like soccer and basketball, as well as fun games such as tag and relay races. Additionally, students participate in exercises that enhance flexibility and coordination, such as stretching and obstacle courses. Dance and rhythm activities are also included to improve balance and rhythm. Overall, these activities aim to build physical fitness, social skills, and a love for active living.
